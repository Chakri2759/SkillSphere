Frequently Asked Questions

What is this platform about?

This platform is designed to help you track your learning progress, manage courses, and improve your skills efficiently.

Can I customize my learning path?

Yes, you can customize your learning path by adding or rearranging courses and resources within the dashboard. Simply use the "Learning Path Customization" feature to tailor your journey according to your goals.

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in a course by visiting the Courses section and selecting the course you're interested in. Click on the "Enroll" button to get started.

Can I track my progress?

Yes, you can track your progress on the Dashboard where you'll find detailed graphs and statistics about your learning journey.
